🗡️ Rogue Class 🗡️

"Masters of stealth, precision, and disruption."

🃏 Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Rogue deck focuses on stealth, precision, and disruption to outmaneuver opponents. It thrives on strategic timing and execution, leveraging Stealth to activate powerful combos and evasion to mitigate damage. Cards in the Rogue deck are divided into three categories: Damage, Trickery, and Thievery, each offering unique advantages like direct damage, hand disruption, or resource theft. The Rogue’s gameplay emphasizes clever card play, making use of hidden maneuvers and assassination tactics to dismantle opponents efficiently.

🔍 Description

The Rogue is a swift and cunning class, specializing in stealth, direct damage, and disruptive mechanics. It thrives on high-damage bursts, trickery, and resource denial, making it a formidable opponent in one-on-one skirmishes.

🃏 How to Play

The Rogue thrives on timing and precision. Early game focuses on establishing Stealth and setting up for powerful assassination moves. Mid-game, disrupt your opponent’s strategy while positioning yourself for high-damage combos. Late-game, execute devastating finishing moves while using your utility cards to maintain control. The Rogue excels at sabotaging and exploiting weaknesses and rewards players who can adapt their strategies to changing circumstances.

⚖️ Pros and Cons

✅ Pros:

  • 💥 High single-target damage potential.
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Strong utility with stealth mechanics and disruptive effects.
  • 🔗 Excellent at controlling enemy resources through discard and AP reduction.

⚠️ Cons:

  • ❌ Low durability; fragile if caught out of stealth.
  • ❌ Relies on setup and timing for maximum effectiveness.
  • ❌ Limited area-of-effect options, making it weaker against multiple enemies.

📊 Class Stats

  • ⚔️ Offense: 9/10 - Exceptional single-target damage with assassination-focused cards.
  • 🛡️ Defense: 3/10 - Minimal defense options; relies on stealth and evasion to avoid damage.
  • 🎯 Utility: 7/10 - Disruption effects and stealth mechanics provide strong utility.
  • 🧠 Complexity: 8/10 - Requires precise timing and resource management for optimal play.
  • 🔗 Synergy: 8/10 - Stealth-based abilities and resource denial cards work seamlessly together.
  • ⏳ Tempo: 8/10 - Fast-paced, with the ability to deliver quick bursts of damage.
  • ⚠️ Risk: 7/10 - High-risk, high-reward gameplay; vulnerable when stealth is unavailable.

📜 Class Guide

🏁 Early Game:

Focus: Establish Stealth and disrupt the opponent’s tempo.
Tips: Build momentum by cycling through cards that generate AP or force the opponent to discard.

⚔️ Mid Game:

Focus: Transition to high-damage attacks while using Stealth to position yourself advantageously.
Tips: Combine utility cards like Smoke Bomb with assassination techniques to maintain control.

🔥 Late Game:

Focus: Execute finishing moves like Assassinate and Silent Death.
Tips: Save your most powerful cards for critical moments. Cards like All In and Silent Death can turn the tide when used strategically.

🎮 Playstyle Tips

  • 🕵️‍♂️ Master Stealth Mechanics: Use Stealth for survival and damage. Cards like Vanish are crucial.
  • 🛡️ Control Resources: Disrupt opponents with discard and AP reduction effects like Sabotage.
  • 💥 Burst Damage Timing: Pair Death Mark with Shadow Strike for devastating combos.
  • ⚔️ Adapt to Opponents: Exploit weaknesses using utility cards to maintain control.
