Rogue

Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Rogue deck focuses on stealth, precision, and disruption to outmaneuver opponents. It thrives on strategic timing and execution, leveraging Stealth to activate powerful combos and evasion to mitigate damage. Cards in the Rogue deck are divided into three categories: Damage, Trickery, and Thievery, each offering unique advantages like direct damage, hand disruption, or resource theft. The Rogue’s gameplay emphasizes clever card play, making use of hidden maneuvers and assassination tactics to dismantle opponents efficiently.

Description

The Rogue is a swift and cunning class, specializing in stealth, direct damage, and disruptive mechanics. It thrives on high-damage bursts, trickery, and resource denial, making it a formidable opponent in one-on-one skirmishes.

Pros:

High single-target damage potential.
Strong utility with stealth mechanics and disruptive effects.
Excellent at controlling enemy resources through discard and AP reduction.

Cons:

Low durability; fragile if caught out of stealth.
Relies on setup and timing for maximum effectiveness.
Limited area-of-effect options, making it weaker against multiple enemies.

Class Guide

Early Game:

Focus: Establish stealth and set up for powerful assassination moves. Use resource denial cards like Cutpurse to disrupt the opponent’s tempo.
Tips: Build momentum by cycling through cards that generate AP or force the opponent to discard.

Mid Game:

Focus: Transition to high-damage attacks like Shadow Strike and Critical Precision. Use stealth-based abilities to position yourself advantageously.
Tips: Combine utility cards like Smoke Bomb with assassination techniques to maintain control over the battlefield.

Late Game:

Focus: Execute finishing moves like Assassinate and Silent Death to close out the game. Use stealth to avoid retaliation and maximize your final strikes.
Tips: Save your most powerful cards for critical moments. Cards like All In and Silent Death can turn the tide when used strategically.

Playstyle Tips:

Master Stealth Mechanics: The Rogue relies heavily on stealth for survival and damage. Cards like Vanish and Silent Approach are crucial for maintaining your edge.
Control the Enemy’s Resources: Use discard and AP reduction effects to disrupt your opponent’s strategy. Cards like Sabotage and Suffocating Trap can cripple their plans.
Burst Damage Timing: Save high-damage cards for pivotal turns. Pair Death Mark with Shadow Strike for devastating combos.
Adapt to Opponents: The Rogue excels at exploiting weaknesses. Use your utility cards to adapt to different scenarios and maintain control.
