Paladin

Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Paladin deck embodies divine balance, combining offense, defense, and support through three unique active slots: Aura System, Judgment and Vows, and Faith and Divine Intercession. Auras provide passive benefits to allies or penalties to enemies, while Judgments and Vows establish criteria for powerful effects. Faith and Divine Intercession cards invoke godly blessings, offering strong buffs or game-changing utilities. The Paladin’s gameplay focuses on maintaining equilibrium, using divine abilities to protect allies, punish foes, and adapt to any situation.

Description

The Paladin is a hybrid class that blends divine power and martial strength, excelling at both defense and support. With powerful healing abilities and holy attacks, the Paladin is a beacon of resilience and hope on the battlefield.

Pros:

Exceptional survivability with strong defensive and healing abilities.
Holy damage mechanics are effective against a variety of enemies.
Can protect and support allies through auras and blessings.

Cons:

Relatively low burst damage compared to pure damage-dealing classes.
Resource-intensive, requiring careful management of Holy Essence and AP.
Vulnerable to being overwhelmed without allies to support.

Class Guide

Early Game:

Focus: Establish defenses and protect allies. Use cards like Aura of Protection to shield your team.
Tips: Generate Holy Essence early by using utility cards like Vow of Resilience to set up future plays.

Mid Game:

Focus: Begin healing and supporting allies while dealing consistent holy damage with cards like Judgment Strike.
Tips: Manage AP carefully to balance offense and support. Don’t overextend without proper defensive setups.

Late Game:

Focus: Use powerful abilities like Divine Wrath to deal massive damage to marked foes or protect allies in critical moments.
Tips: Prioritize keeping allies alive during high-pressure turns. Cards like Aura of Valor can turn the tide of battle.

Playstyle Tips:

Balance Roles: Switch between offense and defense as the situation demands. Don’t hesitate to heal or shield allies even if it means delaying your attacks.
Holy Essence Management: Many of your most powerful abilities require Holy Essence. Plan your plays to generate and utilize it effectively.
Target Marking: Use cards like Judgment of Light to mark key targets, amplifying your damage and utility effects.
Support Strategically: Protect fragile allies and focus on healing priority targets to maintain momentum.
