🛠️ Artificer Class 💉

"Inventors of ingenuity, masters of prosthetics and gadgets!"

🔍 Description

The Artificer is a biomechanical innovator, utilizing gadgets and prosthetics to dominate the battlefield. Their creativity shines through adaptive strategies and synergistic combinations of mechanical marvels.

🃏 How to Play

The Artificer is a biomechanical innovator, utilizing Gadget Cards, Prosthetic Cards, and Utility Cards to dominate the battlefield. The deck revolves around Prosthetics, which provide powerful, persistent effects but often come with limitations or activation costs, and Gadgets, which offer temporary, versatile effects such as damage, defense, or utility boosts. The Artificer’s unique mechanic, Overclock, allows you to exceed your Action Point (AP) limit, but at the cost of taking damage for every AP spent beyond the maximum.

To succeed, you must balance resource management, tactical decision-making, and risk-taking. Start by equipping key Prosthetics to scale your abilities and establish a solid foundation. Use Utility Cards to adapt to threats, such as disrupting opponents or drawing additional cards. As the game progresses, deploy Scaling Gadgets to enhance your active prosthetics or execute devastating combos. Finally, unleash your most powerful effects to turn the tide of battle.

The Artificer rewards players who think several steps ahead, synergizing gadgets and prosthetics for exponential power while mitigating the risks of overextending.

⚖️ Pros and Cons

  • Pros:
    🟢 Incredible versatility with prosthetic customization.
    🟢 Scales well with active gadgets, boosting efficiency and effectiveness.
    🟢 Strong balance of offense, defense, and utility options.
  • Cons:
    🔴 Requires careful resource and AP management.
    🔴 Vulnerable if overloaded with prosthetics beyond AP capabilities.
    🔴 Dependent on synergizing gadgets for optimal performance.

📊 Class Stats

  • 💥 Offense: 7/10 - Consistent damage through offensive prosthetics and scaling abilities.
  • 🛡️ Defense: 8/10 - Gadgets provide robust protective capabilities.
  • 🔧 Utility: 9/10 - Versatile tools and effects provide exceptional utility.
  • 🧠 Complexity: 9/10 - Requires tactical planning and awareness of synergy.
  • 🤝 Synergy: 10/10 - Gadgets and prosthetics scale with one another seamlessly.
  • ⏳ Tempo: 6/10 - Slower start but overwhelming once set up.
  • ⚠️ Risk: 7/10 - Risky if overloading AP and resources.

📜 Class Guide

🏁 Early Game:

Focus: Establish a balance of offense and defense with key prosthetics. Use cards like Power Arm and Tactical Eye for early control.

🏹 Mid Game:

Focus: Scale prosthetics to dominate with synergistic effects. Deploy scaling cards like Elemental Core and Adaptive Frame to enhance all active prosthetics.

🏆 Late Game:

Focus: Overwhelm opponents with fully augmented strategies. Leverage cards like Overclock Surge and Cyber Domination for devastating effects.

🎮 Playstyle Tips

  • 🔄 Prosthetic Management: Balance permanent and temporary prosthetics to maximize efficiency while avoiding AP overload.
  • ⚖️ Adapt to the Battlefield: Use utility cards like Tinker’s Mastery to respond to evolving threats.
  • 🔗 Synergize for Impact: Combine prosthetics with scaling effects for exponential power.
  • ⏱️ Mitigate Risk: Avoid overextending AP to maintain a stable setup.
  • ❌ Dominate with Control: Use cards like Overload Amplifier to disrupt opponents while solidifying your position.
