Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Duelist deck focuses on precision, agility, and calculated strikes, utilizing the unique Flow Mechanic. Flow is activated by playing a card that is not the first in a turn, triggering additional effects or follow-ups. This mechanic rewards strategic planning and adaptability, allowing Duelists to chain abilities together for devastating combos. Gameplay revolves around maintaining Flow, countering opponentsâ€™ actions, and exploiting openings to outmaneuver and dismantle foes with finesse.

Description

The Duelist thrives in precision and fluidity, excelling at chaining strikes for devastating combos. With the Flow System, each card played builds momentum, enhancing the effects of subsequent actions, making them a true master of calculated aggression.

Pros:

High burst damage potential through strategic card chaining.
Flexible playstyle with a balance of offense and utility.
Combos offer opportunities for creative and adaptive strategies.

Cons:

Vulnerable if interrupted mid-combo.
Requires careful planning to optimize the Flow System.
Lacks significant defense, relying on quick counters and timing.

Class Guide

Early Game:

Focus: Build momentum with opening strikes and grip effects.
Tips: Use cards like Piercing Lunge and Momentum Shift to establish Flow.

Mid Game:

Focus: Chain strikes and grips for maximum impact.
Tips: Save Precision Strike and Tempo Surge for critical turns to maintain momentum.

Late Game:

Focus: Unleash devastating combos with Final Stand cards.
Tips: Use cards like Evasive Parry to interrupt enemy actions and secure your Flow advantage.

Playstyle Tips:

Master the Flow: Plan your turns to maximize the bonus effects of your second, third, or fourth cards.
Adapt Your Combos: Tailor your sequences based on enemy vulnerabilities and current resources.
Counter with Precision: Use quick cards like Riposte to punish overaggressive enemies.
Grips for Advantage: Employ cards like Control Hold to set up favorable conditions for your strikes.
Capitalize on Final Stands: Save Crescendo or Unstoppable Duel for the perfect moment to overwhelm opponents.
