Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Warlock deck thrives on high-risk, high-reward strategies, wielding curses, life-draining spells, and devastating apocalyptic effects. The deck introduces Spell Slots, allowing players to keep up to five active spells on the battlefield, each with limited uses. Many Warlock cards require sacrificing HP or discarding cards, emphasizing resource management and tactical decision-making. Gameplay revolves around building momentum through curses and life taps while timing devastating effects to overwhelm opponents and seize control of the battlefield.

Description

The Warlock thrives on high-risk, high-reward gameplay, leveraging curses, life-draining effects, and devastating rituals. By sacrificing their own resources, they unleash powerful abilities to weaken enemies and sustain themselves.

Pros:

Powerful curses for debilitating enemies.
Life tap mechanics provide self-healing and buffs.
High-impact spells with devastating effects in the Apocalypse category.

Cons:

Relies heavily on sacrificing HP and cards, creating a fragile balance.
Vulnerable to burst damage when resources are low.
High-risk gameplay may backfire if not managed carefully.
