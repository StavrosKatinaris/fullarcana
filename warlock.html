🔮 Warlock Class 🔮

"Masters of curses, life-draining spells, and devastating apocalyptic power."

🌟 Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Warlock deck thrives on high-risk, high-reward strategies, wielding curses, life-draining spells, and devastating apocalyptic effects. The deck introduces Spell Slots, allowing players to keep up to five active spells on the battlefield, each with limited uses. Many Warlock cards require sacrificing HP or discarding cards, emphasizing resource management and tactical decision-making. Gameplay revolves around building momentum through curses and life taps while timing devastating effects to overwhelm opponents and seize control of the battlefield.

🔍 Description

The Warlock wields the power of death 💀 and chaos, summoning devastating curses 🕷️ and siphoning life to sustain themselves. Their high-risk, high-reward playstyle is perfect for players who thrive on calculated sacrifices and overwhelming power.

🃏 How to Play

The Warlock relies on managing Spell Slots and leveraging curses to dominate the battlefield. Each turn, you can place up to five active spells in your Spell Slots, enabling sustained effects or devastating one-time abilities. Sacrificing HP and cards is essential to power your apocalyptic spells, but balance is critical to avoid leaving yourself vulnerable. Early game, focus on setting curses to weaken enemies and building resources. Mid-game, sustain yourself with life taps while maintaining constant pressure. Late-game, unleash devastating combos and high-cost spells to overwhelm your opponent and seize victory.

⚖️ Pros and Cons

✅ Pros:

  • 💥 Devastating damage potential with curses and life-draining spells.
  • 🔄 Exceptional resource manipulation through sacrifices and discard effects.
  • 🕸️ Strong control mechanics to debilitate opponents.

⚠️ Cons:

  • ❌ Fragile if not managed carefully, relying heavily on self-sacrifice.
  • ❌ High-risk gameplay can backfire without proper planning.
  • ❌ Vulnerable to burst damage when resources are depleted.

📜 Class Guide

🏁 Early Game:

Focus: Establish curses and generate resources. Use low-cost cards to build momentum while conserving HP.
Tips: Apply consistent pressure while preparing for stronger spells mid-game.

⚔️ Mid Game:

Focus: Sustain yourself with life taps while applying constant pressure. Leverage curses to debilitate enemies.
Tips: Balance HP sacrifices with resource generation to avoid overextending.

🔥 Late Game:

Focus: Unleash apocalyptic spells and devastating combos to overwhelm opponents.
Tips: Save high-impact cards for pivotal moments to turn the tide in your favor.

🎮 Playstyle Tips

  • ⚖️ Balance Resources: Carefully manage HP, AP, and cards to maintain momentum.
  • 🕷️ Apply Curses Early: Set the stage for powerful combos by debuffing opponents.
  • ✴️ Leverage Spell Slots: Keep a mix of sustained effects and explosive spells active.
  • 🔮 Time Your Ultimates: Save high-cost spells for critical moments to maximize impact.
