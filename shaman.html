🌪️ Shaman Class 🌪️

"Masters of nature’s fury and spiritual balance."

⛈️ Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Shaman deck channels the forces of nature and the spiritual realm, leveraging Weather Effects and Spiritual Totems to shape the battlefield. The Shaman has a single Weather Slot, where Weather Manipulation cards remain active and can only be replaced by another Weather Manipulation card—they cannot be discarded otherwise. Weather Effects create global changes that impact all players, while Spiritual Totems provide passive buffs or trigger specific effects. Gameplay revolves around maintaining control through strategic weather manipulation and spiritual synergy to outlast or overwhelm foes.

🔍 Description

The Shaman is a master of the elements and a conduit for the spiritual realm, using weather manipulation and spirit-guided powers to control the battlefield. Their strength lies in their ability to adapt to any situation, providing support and disruption through powerful natural forces.

🃏 How to Play

The Shaman uses their single Weather Slot to create impactful effects across the battlefield, influencing all players. Strategic totem placement amplifies their power, allowing them to control the flow of battle. Early game, focus on weather control and setting up your totems. Mid-game, use synergy between spirits and weather to dominate the board. Late-game, unleash Legendary Invocations to secure victory.

⚖️ Pros and Cons

✅ Pros:

  • 🌩️ Unique weather effects that influence all players.
  • 🛡️ Strong balance of offensive and defensive capabilities.
  • 🌟 Adaptable playstyle that suits both solo and group strategies.

⚠️ Cons:

  • ❌ Reliant on weather manipulation, which can affect allies as well as enemies.
  • ❌ Requires strategic foresight to maximize spirit and weather synergy.
  • ❌ Vulnerable to fast, high-burst damage.

📊 Class Stats

  • ⚡ Offense: 8/10 - Elemental spirits and weather effects deal solid damage with persistent effects.
  • 🛡️ Defense: 9/10 - Totems and weather tools provide excellent protective abilities.
  • ✨ Utility: 9/10 - Highly versatile with buffs, debuffs, and battlefield control.
  • 🧠 Complexity: 8/10 - Strategic and dynamic, requiring careful planning and timing.
  • 🔗 Synergy: 10/10 - Outstanding in group play with buffs, healing, and shared effects.
  • ⏳ Tempo: 7/10 - Moderate tempo, with gradual build-up of powerful effects.
  • ⚠️ Risk: 6/10 - Relatively safe, with defensive tools to mitigate risks.

📜 Class Guide

🏁 Early Game:

Focus: Establish weather control and summon totems to support your strategy.
Tips: Use cards like Chant of the Gale to disrupt enemy actions while preparing your defenses.

⚔️ Mid Game:

Focus: Use spirits and weather synergy to control the flow of battle and apply consistent pressure.
Tips: Combine spirits like Spirit of the Wolf with weather cards such as Heavy Rain for layered effects.

🔥 Late Game:

Focus: Leverage powerful Legendary Invocations and weather tools to overwhelm enemies or protect allies.
Tips: Use high-impact cards like Thunderstorm Invocation to turn the tide of battle.

🎮 Playstyle Tips

  • 🌩️ Weather Manipulation: Choose weather effects carefully to disrupt enemies while minimizing harm to allies.
  • 🐻 Spirit Synergy: Use spirit cards like Spirit of the Bear to amplify your elemental powers and create layered strategies.
  • 🛡️ Totem Placement: Deploy totems to provide buffs or debuffs that align with your game plan.
  • 🌪️ Adaptability: Balance offensive and defensive actions based on the battlefield state.
  • ⏳ Timing Matters: Save Legendary Invocations like Sunrise Invocation for game-defining turns.
