🎵 Bard Class 🎵

"Compose melodies, inspire allies, and control the battlefield!"

🎼 Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Bard deck revolves around composing melodies using Note Cards from various music genres, each with unique effects. Players gradually craft songs that can be "released" as a quick or normal action for powerful effects. Each genre corresponds to a distinct gameplay mechanic, offering buffs, debuffs, or situational advantages. The Bard’s gameplay emphasizes creativity and synergy, rewarding strategic composition and timing to influence the battlefield and support allies or hinder foes.

🔍 Description

The Bard is a versatile and supportive class, weaving melodies to buff allies, debuff enemies, and disrupt the battlefield. Through their musical mastery, Bards can adapt to any situation, excelling at synergy and creative play.

🃏 How to Play

The Bard is a creative and adaptive class, using Note Cards (C, D, E, F, G) to compose powerful Songs that provide diverse effects. Each Note Card belongs to one of 10 musical categories:

  • Pop: Healing effects.
  • Rock: Physical damage.
  • Jazz: Utility and AP manipulation.
  • Classical: Defense and buffs.
  • Heavy Metal: Debuffs.
  • Country: Self-sustain.
  • Electronic: Movement and speed enhancements.
  • Blues: Damage-over-time (DoT).
  • Folk: Summons and ally support.
  • Punk: Discard and hand manipulation.

The Bard’s unique mechanic, Song Composition, involves playing Note Cards onto the board to create an array of cards, forming a Song. Notes gain additional effects depending on the sequence of previous Notes in the Song (e.g., "If the previous Notes are C, F, heals 5 HP to the target"). The Bard can compose multiple Songs simultaneously and play them as either Normal Actions or Quick Actions, depending on their strategic needs.

⚖️ Pros and Cons

✅ Pros:

  • 🎵 Highly flexible with buffs, debuffs, and utility effects.
  • 🎵 Excellent at enhancing allies and weakening enemies.
  • 🎵 Unique note-combo system creates high-synergy opportunities.

⚠️ Cons:

  • ❌ Requires setup and strategic planning to maximize note combinations.
  • ❌ Lacks strong direct damage options.
  • ❌ Vulnerable if isolated without allies to support.

📊 Class Stats

  • 🎵 Offense: 5/10 - Limited damage potential, mostly through supporting attacks.
  • 🛡️ Defense: 6/10 - Strong ally-protection abilities but weak self-defense.
  • ✨ Utility: 10/10 - Exceptional buffs, debuffs, and situational adaptability.
  • 🧠 Complexity: 9/10 - Challenging to master due to reliance on note sequencing and synergy.
  • 🔗 Synergy: 10/10 - Thrives in cooperative play, enhancing team effectiveness.
  • ⏳ Tempo: 7/10 - Moderate pace, with bursts of impact through well-timed note combos.
  • ⚠️ Risk: 5/10 - Relatively low-risk as long as the Bard manages resources effectively.

📜 Class Guide

🏁 Early Game:

Focus on building your Songs by placing Note Cards strategically. Use healing genres like Pop or defensive genres like Classical to stabilize the early game.

🎵 Mid Game:

Transition to supporting allies or pressuring opponents with impactful Songs. Use genres like Rock for damage or Jazz for resource manipulation.

🎶 Late Game:

Release your most powerful Songs to amplify team synergy or debilitate opponents. Debuff-heavy genres like Heavy Metal and DoT effects from Blues shine in the late game.

🎮 Playstyle Tips

  • 🔄 Adapt Your Songs: Match your compositions to the flow of battle. Use healing or defense against high-pressure decks and debuffs or DoT against sustain-heavy decks.
  • 🎵 Synergize Notes: Plan Note sequences carefully to trigger additional effects.
  • ⚡ Quick Actions: Save Quick Songs for clutch plays or countering opponents’ moves.
  • ✨ Support Your Allies: Prioritize buffs and healing to maximize team effectiveness in multiplayer modes.
