Description

The Bard is a versatile and supportive class, weaving melodies to buff allies, debuff enemies, and disrupt the battlefield. Through their musical mastery, Bards can adapt to any situation, excelling at synergy and creative play.

Pros:

Highly flexible with buffs, debuffs, and utility effects.
Excellent at enhancing allies and weakening enemies.
Unique note-combo system creates high-synergy opportunities.

Cons:

Requires setup and strategic planning to maximize note combinations.
Lacks strong direct damage options.
Vulnerable if isolated without allies to support.

Class Guide

Early Game:

Focus: Establish note combinations while protecting allies.
Tips: Use utility cards like Prelude of Harmony to set up combos for future turns.

Mid Game:

Focus: Start buffing allies and debuffing enemies to shift the battlefield in your favor.
Tips: Play cards like Melody of Courage to strengthen allies while disrupting enemy strategies.

Late Game:

Focus: Use powerful note-combo effects to unleash impactful buffs or crippling debuffs.
Tips: Save high-synergy cards like Symphony of Triumph for critical moments to turn the tide.

Playstyle Tips:

Note Sequencing: Plan your notes carefully to trigger powerful combo effects.
Support Allies: Prioritize enhancing allies with cards like Chant of Renewal to keep your team strong.
Disrupt Enemies: Use debuffs like Disharmony Strike to weaken enemies and control the board.
Adaptability: Stay flexible, using your wide range of utility to respond to changing scenarios.
Team Synergy: Coordinate with allies to maximize the impact of your buffs and utility.
