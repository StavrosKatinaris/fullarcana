Alchemist

Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Alchemist deck revolves around Potion Crafting, combining Ingredient Cards to create powerful Potions. Ingredients provide small, individual effects but must be played without using their effects to craft Potions, which are then activated for healing, damage, buffs, or utility. Once infused into a Potion, Ingredients are returned to the card pile, ensuring resource recycling. The Alchemist can store 1-2 completed Potions for strategic use. Gameplay emphasizes careful ingredient management, crafting impactful Potions, and timing their use to control the battlefield effectively.

Description

The Alchemist is a master of concoctions and transmutations, using potions and elixirs to heal, empower, and devastate. With an emphasis on crafting and adaptability, they thrive on creative combinations and versatile effects.

Pros:

Highly flexible with a wide array of effects.
Potions offer powerful healing, buffs, and damage.
Adaptable playstyle tailored to different scenarios.

Cons:

Requires strategic planning for potion crafting and usage.
Limited direct offensive capability outside potions.
Vulnerable to disruption or fast-paced opponents.

Class Guide

Focus: Craft foundational potions for healing, buffs, and moderate damage.
Tips: Use cards like Elixir of Vitality to establish sustainability early.

Mid Game:

Focus: Expand your arsenal of potions and use them strategically to counter threats.
Tips: Combine damage and debuff potions like Acid Flask with healing potions for a balanced approach.
