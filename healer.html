Healer

Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

Deck Mechanics and Gameplay

The Healer deck excels at sustaining allies and countering damage through potent healing, buffs, and strategic utility. The deck introduces the Holy Essence resource, which is generated by specific cards and empowers others to deliver enhanced effects. Healers must balance restoring health, applying buffs to fortify allies, and removing debuffs from the battlefield. With a mix of healing spells, Holy Damage, and utility cards, the Healer’s gameplay revolves around resourceful planning and supporting their strategy while occasionally turning defense into offense.

Description

The Healer is a vital support class, specializing in restoring health, mitigating damage, and enhancing allies' capabilities. With a balance of utility and sustain, the Healer ensures their team can outlast any opponent.

Pros:

Excellent healing and sustain capabilities.
Strong support options for boosting allies' effectiveness.
Flexible playstyle with a mix of defensive and utility cards.

Cons:

Limited offensive potential; struggles in prolonged one-on-one duels.
Relies heavily on allies to maximize impact.
Vulnerable when focused, especially if overwhelmed by burst damage.

Class Guide

Early Game:

Focus: Establish healing and defensive buffs. Use cards like Renewing Light to restore early health while setting up defensive measures.
Tips: Prioritize cards that boost healing output or protect key allies.

Mid Game:

Focus: Balance healing and utility. Cards like Blessing of Resilience and Aura of Vitality keep allies alive while setting up buffs.
Tips: Time your healing strategically. Overhealing wastes resources, so assess the battlefield carefully.

Late Game:

Focus: Maximize healing output while amplifying ally power. Use high-impact cards like Divine Rejuvenation to turn the tide of battle.
Tips: Prioritize survival. If the Healer is eliminated, allies will struggle to maintain momentum.

Playstyle Tips:

Resource Management: Healing can be resource-intensive. Balance AP usage between healing and utility to avoid running out of options.
Support Synergy: Pair healing with buffs to maximize ally effectiveness. For example, use Empowered Blessing to increase an ally's offensive output after healing them.
Timing is Key: Save high-impact cards like Holy Intervention for critical moments. React quickly to unexpected damage spikes.
Positioning: Keep the Healer protected. Cards like Guardian’s Shield can help mitigate damage to ensure survivability.
